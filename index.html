About Me

Hi! I am a Bioinformatician Scientist at Gujarat Biotechnology Research Centre (GBRC), India. My research focuses on microbial metagenomics to study microbial evolution, transmission, host-pathogen interactions to support the One Health framework. Furthermore, I am also deeply interested in Computational Biology, Statistics, Generative AI, and Deep Learning.

Beyond bioinformatics, I enjoy exploring Gujarati literature, scouting new culinary ideas, and occasionally challenging the status quo.
